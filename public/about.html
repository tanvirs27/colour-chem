About COLOUR CHEM

Md Rubel Akter Rana
CEO, COLOUR CHEM

 

COLOUR CHEM was established in 2012.

Now COLOUR CHEM is the largest Dyestuff & Chemicals supplier and technical service provider for the Bangladesh Textile Industry.

We are serving customers mainly engaged in

–    Knit & Woven Fabric Dyeing & Finishing
–    Knit & Woven Fabric Printing with Dyestuff & Pigment
–    Yarn Dyeing & Finishing
–    Garments Printing & Decoration
–    Garments Washing & Dyeing
–    Denim fabric manufacturing

COLOUR CHEM is the pioneer to introduce new technologies for the Knit-Dyeing, Garments Washing and Garments Printing Industries in Bangladesh. Expert Technical support and Extensive Laboratory services have been the key to its growth.

COLOUR CHEM provides wide range of dyes & chemicals, from preparation to finishing, through a countrywide distribution network of sales & marketing offices and bulk warehouses. COLOUR CHEM has built-up large stock around the country for easy availability to its customers to save valuable lead time. We also issue indents for those customers who want to import in bulk quantity or for those who enjoy duty-free import facility.

COLOUR CHEM provides technical consultation to customers, with the help of our ever-improving laboratory facilities. We have a high-tech Laboratory which can fulfill the dyeing, printing & testing requirement of a Modern Industry.  
COLOUR CHEM has its own product development & application research facilities. We source products according to the quality requirements of our industry.

We maintain close relation with buying houses, to update each other with latest technologies and end-user requirements, which we communicate with our customers and suppliers. We maintain assurance for buyers and factories to meet eco-logical requirements.

Now COLOUR CHEM is considered to be the one-stop-shop for Dyestuff, Chemicals, Screen Printing Consumables and Testing Equipment. We came this far with our only goal: Customer Satisfaction. And we believe, COLOUR CHEM will continue to create a prosperous future for its customers.
