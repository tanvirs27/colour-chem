Accredited for ISO/IEC 17025 by Bangladesh Accreditation Board (BAB)

Colour Chem Textile Testing Laboratory is accredited by Bangladesh Accreditation Board (BAB) on 30th June, 2014 for ISO/IEC 17025. ISO/IEC 17025 defines what is required of a laboratory in order for it to demonstrate both the technical competence of the personnel and the availability of all technical resources needed to produce accurate and reliable data and results for a defined set of tests, measurements or calibrations. Laboratories use ISO/IEC 17025 to implement a quality system aimed at improving their ability to consistently produce valid results.

ISO/IEC 17025 also has a component to it that requires that the management systems of the facility meet the principles of ISO 9001. A laboratory’s fulfillment of the requirements of ISO/IEC 17025 means the laboratory meets both the requirements at technical competence and management systems, which are necessary for it to deliver technically valid test results and calibrations.
