Machinery Division

Colour Chem Started promoting Laboratory Dyeing & Finishing equipment since 1990 and expanded range of machinery brands after 2001.

Now we are offering full range of Laboratory & Testing Equipment for Textile Industry. Other major equipment sold is Printing Equipment for Textile & Garment.

We are having a customer base of more than 300 textile factories using equipment from Colour Chem.

Our reputation is built on strong after-sales technical service, and we enjoy many repeat orders due to such goodwill.

Laboratory & Testing Equipment for Textile Industry
