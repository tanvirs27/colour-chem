From HUBEI HWALLE DYESTUFF INDUSTRY CO., LTD. (CHUYUAN GROUP), HUBEI, CHINA (Largest Reactive Dyes Manufacturer in the world)

• Hubei Chuyuan Group is one of the largest chemicals group company in Chinca, which mainly produces dyestuffs, dyestuffs intermediate, fertilizers, pesticides and pharmaceuticals.

• Since 1990, it has been producing intermediates for Reactive Dyes and established itself as the World’s largest production unit for H-acid, K-acid and Para base. By producing 8 (eight) main intermediates of Reactive dyes, it is presently controlling about 60% of the global intermediate market for reactive dyes.

• Hubei Hwalle Dyestuff Industry Co., Ltd. who is a daughter company of Hubei Chuyuan Group, mainly produces Reactive dyestuff.

• It has the most sophisticated automatic line of production and quality assurance systems, run by set of a local & foreign expert team for both dyestuffs intermediate and finished dyes.

• It invests substantial amount of revenue in the continuous research & development.

• It is the largest OEM supplier for Reactive dyestuff to a number of world famous multinationals.

• It is the world’s largest producer of Reactive dyes with a present production capacity of 70,000 MT per year.

• All times are eco-friendly (OEKO-TEX standard 100).

• In 2004, Colour Chem-Chem Ltd., signed a long term contract with Chuyuan Group to Manufacturer a range of high quality Reactive dyes for Bangladesh market under “DYCHUFIX” and became Sole distributor for Bangladesh.

DISPERSE DYES

JIANGSU YABANG DYESTUFF CO., LTD was initiated and sponsored by YABANG CHEMICAL GROUP and set up in 2006 based on previous YABANG dyestuff factories as a joint stock company aiming to be listed in the market. The company’s headquarters are located in Changzhou city of Jiangsu province, which is one major city in the Yangzi River Delta region. The company occupies an area of 750 thousand square meters with gross asset over 1 Billion RMB and more than 2500 employees. The company has a R&D and QA Division comprising of dozens of Ph.D holders. The company has set up several production bases in places such as Jiangsu Changzhou, Lianyungang, Haimen, Anhui Tongling etc, and possesses two branches and three subsidiaries.

The company mainly produces dyestuffs and intermediates. It is the biggest production base in China for Anthraquinone series and Intermediates. Their production and sales volume of 1 Amino AQ, Disperse Red 60 & 92, Disperse Blue 60, Disperse Yellow 54, Vat Blue 54, Vat Blue 4, Vat Blue 6 and Vat Black 25 are ranking first in the global market. In 2008 the sales volume of Yabang reached 2 Billion RMB.

Yabang is the strategic cooperative partner of several world renowned multinational companies. Disperse dyestuff with the trademark of Yabang is the only well-known Brand in the China Dyestuff Industry. In 2008 Yabang was accredited as the National High-Tech Enterprise and as “China’s Chemical Science and Technology Innovation Model Enterprise” by the China Petroleum Chemical Association. Yabang dyestuff is now enjoying a high profile and reputation in the dyestuff Industry both in China and abroad.

Colour Chem-Chem Ltd., the leading Dyes & Chemical Distributor & Technical Service Provider in Bangladesh Textile Industry, is exclusively distributing the Disperse & Vat dyestuff from Yabang since year 2008. The high performance Disperse Dyes from Yabang can fulfill the performance requirement of High-Street Fashion Retailers of Europe, USA and other markets.
