Technical Services

DYSIN's core business depends on strong Sales & Distribution network backed by efficient technical services. We believe in providing Solutions, not Products only.

We are one of the first textile chemical companies in Bangladesh to provide laboratory services since the beginning of 90's. We are also the first to provide computerized color matching services using Spectrophotometer and Automatic Laboratory Dispensing System since 1998.

Now our laboratories provide recipe prediction service to customers together with performance parameters like colorfastness & physical properties. We also use these laboratories to provide training & demonstration to our customers as well as own staff.

In order to provide internationally recognized testing result, our laboratories are now in the process of acquiring ISO17025 accreditation.

Laboratory Services
Laboratory for Recipe Prediction and Sample Development

This Laboratory is used for Dyeing, Printing & Finishing Recipe Preparation in Exhaust, Pad-Batch & Continuous process; having

  • Spectrophotometer Color Matching Service using Spectrophotometers from X-Rite USA & Konica Minolta Japan
  • Automatic Tubeless Dispensing System & Solution Maker from Taiwan
  • More than eight Sample Dyeing Machines from Taiwan
  • Padding Mangle & HT-Steamer from Taiwan
Laboratory for Colorfastness, Physical Properties & Analytical Testing

This Laboratory is used to perform major tests for colorfastness & physical properties, eg. Colorfastness to Light, Crocking, Washing, Perspiration, Heat Sublimation etc. The major equipment in the laboratory is as below:

  • Q-Sun Xenon Lightfastness Tester from USA
  • Standard Garment Washing & Drying Equipment for ISO & AATCC
  • Universal Strength Tester
  • Pilling & Abrasion Tester
  • Formaldehyde Tester, & more...

Training Programs and Technical Service team
Technical Training Programs for Industry Individuals

Knowledge grows by sharing, and we are keen to grow ours. This is why we always welcome Professionals and students for training at our place. Technical training programs and workshops are regularly arranged to update industry professionals with latest technologies and innovations. We allow Textile University & colleges to use our laboratories for their practical courses or external projects.


AATCC International Training Program

As per the agreement with American Association of Textile Chemists & Colorists (AATCC), DYSIN is the first place in Bangladesh to provide the opportunity to become an AATCC Certified Test Method Technologist. In the DYSIN Conference Center & Testing Lab at the corporate head office, the AATCC International Training Program on Colorfastness & Physical Properties is organized on a regular basis and the Certificate is issued by AATCC, USA.


Technical Team for Service at Production houses

As said before, we believe in providing Solutions, not Products only. Our technical personnel are always sharing their expertise with customers, helping them to achieve better results from their production through identification of vital points in the process.

Demonstration & Training Unit for Garments Printing

Since the year 2000 we have a demonstration unit of Garments Printing with fully equipped setup for various kinds of specialty prints on T-Shirt. It has the following major equipment:

  • Automatic 10 color Printing Carousel from Turkey
  • Conveyor Dryer from USA
  • Flocking Equipment from Germany
  • Flat Screen Printer for Transfer Print from Taiwan.

This Printing unit is used for training & demonstration of products.


Screen Making Unit for customer service

Since 1999 Dysin provides Silk Screen Stretching, Coating and Developing Services with fully automatic Japanese Equipment to serve Garments Printing Industries with high quality screens.

Stretching table with automatic pneumatic stretcher clamps, Exposing machine with Arc Xenon Lamps, Automatic Drying Oven, Wash & Developing stand etc. are the main attractions of this high-tech establishment.
